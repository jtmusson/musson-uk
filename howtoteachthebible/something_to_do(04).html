Something to do (4)

John 13:1-17 again.

Q3. What is the application for us today?

The principle we are concentrating on here is that Christians should serve one another. Remember that application means that we are looking for practical examples.


Imagine you are teaching pensioners. What might be some possible applications?

Here are some suggestions:
You could think of pensioners who live near you who need help: you might offer to go shopping with them, or, if they are ill or very weak, to do their shopping for them.
You could ask some families or singles in the church how you could pray for them regularly.
If you are physically up to it, you could offer to help with the tea and coffee after services on a Sunday.


Imagine you are teaching teenagers. What might be some practical applications?

Here are some suggestions:
Can you think of someone in your class at school who struggles with maths that you find quite easy? Although they are not really a friend of yours, you might offer to spend half an hour helping them to understand something they find hard. You could volunteer to help wash up at the end of the next youth group meeting. Why not astonish your mother by asking if she wants a bit of help with the shopping?!
