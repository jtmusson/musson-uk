Something to do (3)

We are still in John 13:1-17. Your answers may look like something like this:

Q2. What is the universal meaning?

What lessons and principles are there in this text which are true in every culture and in every century?

     Jesus’ lordship includes the humility of servanthood (v3-5)
     We should let Jesus serve us (v8-9)
     When someone has been forgiven by Jesus it is as if they have had a bath (v10)
     Those who are followers of Jesus should serve one another (v14-15)

All of those things are important. But perhaps the one lesson John wants to stress more than all the others is the last, because this is the emphasis of the climax of the passage in verses 12-17.
