Something to do (1)

I cannot tell you what your answers should be here!

But I do want to encourage you to think and pray about who your one or two models of Bible teaching might be.

You might borrow or buy one of John Stott’s books in IVP’s The Bible Speaks Today series. Obviously a book is not the same as a Bible talk, but this may help nevertheless.

Of course you could always ask some friends for suggestions of possible Bible teaching models.
