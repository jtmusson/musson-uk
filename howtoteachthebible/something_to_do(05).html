This is still about John 13:1-17.

Q4. What is the main thought in the passage?

There is so much here that I find it hard to sum it up in one sentence! Here are some suggestions:
Because Jesus our Lord washed his disciples’ feet, we should be willing to serve each other.
If we want to experience God’s blessing, we should imitate Jesus’ servant-hearted service by serving one another.

You may have come up with a better way of putting it!
