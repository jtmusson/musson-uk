Something to do (10)

This suggested conclusion of a Bible talk on John 13:1-17 would be more effective if it was linked with a specific structure-with-headings. 


A possible conclusion

So we have been looking at Jesus: he knows who he is and why he has come.
     He is the Lord.
     So let’s worship him. Let’s thank him for loving us to the end.
     And let’s thank him for his extraordinary love in washing the disciples’ feet.

And we’ve been listening to Jesus too. 
     He has told us to serve one another.
     What is the Holy Spirit saying to you?
     Are there things he is calling you to do in these next few days? 

Jesus is here. Let’s worship him. And let’s obey him. 
Let’s pray together.
