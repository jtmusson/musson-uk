Something to do (7)

I hope you will not look at these suggestions until you have had a go yourself!

a. Mark 8:27-38
  I think the structure of the passage is clear:
       verses 27-30
       verses 31-33
       verses 34-38
   
  Here are some possible headings:
  1. Who Jesus is (27-30)
  2. Why Jesus came (31-33)
  3. What Jesus wants (34-38)
   
  A Christian is someone who...
  1. ...sees who Jesus is (27-30)
  2. ...understands why Jesus came (31-33)
  3. ...does what Jesus wants (34-38)
   
b. Ephesians 2:1-10
  It seems clear that verses 1-3 are a section. But if you have a structure that includes 4-7 and 8-10, my guess is that you found it difficult to find headings.
   
  I think that this is because verse 10 is really a new unit.
   
  In my opinion the most plausible structure is
       verses 1-3
       verses 4-9
       verse 10
   
  but this is also an option:
       verses 1-3
       verses 4-7
       verses 8-9
       verse 10
   
  Here are some possible headings:
  1. What we were (1-3)
  2. What God did (4-9)
  3. What we are (10)
   
  1. Slaves of sin (1-3)
  2. Saved by grace (4-9)
  3. Servants of God (10)
   
  1. Rebels under God’s judgment (1-3)
  2. Saved by God’s Son (4-7)
  3. Transformed through God’s grace (8-9)
  4. Joined to God’s people (10)
   
c. Philippians 1:3-26
  The structure here seems to me to be clear:
       verses 3-11
       verses 12-18a
       verses 18b-26
   
  Some possible headings:
  1. Paul loves Christians (3-11)
  2. Paul loves the Gospel (12-18a)
  3. Paul loves Jesus (18b-26)
   
d. 1 Corinthians 9:19-27
  I think that the structure of this passage has two parts:
       verses 19-23
       verses 24-27
   
  Some possible headings:
  1. Paul’s public ministry (19-23)
  2. Paul’s private morality (24-27)
   
  1. Make yourself people’s slave (19-23)
  2. Make your body your slave (24-27)
   
e. Luke 5:1-11
  It seems to me we are making a mistake if we do not make the last section verses 10b-11. It is clearly a new topic (the calling of the disciples).
   
  So there are two main options here:
       verses 1-3 verses 1-3
       verses 4-10a verses 4-7
       verses 10b-11 verses 8-10a
    verses 10b-11
   
  See what you think of these possible headings:
  Four steps on the way to becoming a Jesus disciple
       1. Hear him (1-3)
       2. Watch him (4-7)
       3. Confess him (8-10a)
       4. Follow him (10b-11)
       (the weak heading here is the third)
 
  1. The bait (1-3)
  2. The fishing (4-7)
  3. The catch (8-11)
 
  Four steps on the way to becoming a Jesus disciple
       1. Listen (1-3)
       2. Obey (4-7)
       3. Repent (8-10a)
       4. Do (10b-11)
       (and the first letters of each heading spell something important!)
 
  1. What Peter hears (1-3)
  2. What Peter sees (4-7)
  3. What Peter feels (8-10a)
  4. What Peter does (10b-11)
