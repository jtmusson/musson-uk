Something to do - 3
I find one-sentence summaries difficult.
I suggest this for Mark 4:35-41. As it’s a narrative passage, this summarises what happens in the story: When the boat Jesus and his disciples are in is caught up in a violent storm, Jesus’ immense authority in suddenly calming the storm causes the disciples to ask themselves who he is.
You can’t get every detail into a summary: if you insist on that you could just as well copy out the whole passage!
I think that summary is OK, but you may well have done a better job.
What won’t do for part of the summary of Mark 4:35-41 is something along the lines of and so the disciples learn that he can calm all the storms we ever meet in life.
See my comments near the end of Something to do (2) for why.
