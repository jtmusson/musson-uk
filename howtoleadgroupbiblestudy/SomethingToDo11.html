How to Lead Group Bible Study
Something to do - 11
You will notice that I have been careful to say that we might not want to actually use the word mentor. But in any case the role is important.
This applies to almost anything: if we don’t have someone who will help us to get better at what we do, it’s very unlikely that we’ll improve.
We will stay stuck in our rut, not aware of our weaknesses and what we can do about them.
